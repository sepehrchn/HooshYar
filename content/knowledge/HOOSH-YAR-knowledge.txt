## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: What industries do you work with?**
A: We work with clients across many industries including e-commerce, SaaS, fintech, healthcare (non-clinical), education, marketing agencies, and startups. Our AI and automation solutions are industry-agnostic.

**Q: Do you work with international clients?**
A: Yes! We work with clients globally. Our team is fluent in English and Persian (Farsi). We accommodate different time zones and use modern collaboration tools (Slack, Zoom, Notion).

**Q: What's the minimum project size?**
A: We take projects starting from $1,000. For smaller needs, we can recommend no-code tools or off-the-shelf solutions.

**Q: Do you offer hourly rates or only fixed-price projects?**
A: We prefer fixed-price for well-defined projects, but we offer hourly rates ($75-$150/hour depending on service) for ongoing retainers or exploratory work.

**Q: Can you maintain an existing website or automation?**
A: Yes. We offer maintenance and support for projects built with our tech stack (Next.js, React, Node.js, etc.). We can also take over projects built by others after a code review.

### AI Services

**Q: Can you integrate AI into my existing website or app?**
A: Yes. We can add AI features (chatbots, content generation, recommendations, etc.) to most existing web applications via API integration.

**Q: Which AI models do you use?**
A: We work with OpenAI (GPT-4, GPT-3.5), Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, and open-source models from Hugging Face. We recommend the best model based on your use case and budget.

**Q: How much does an AI chatbot cost?**
A: Simple chatbot (pre-defined responses): $1,000-$2,000. AI-powered chatbot (custom-trained on your data): $2,000-$5,000+. Ongoing AI API costs (OpenAI, etc.) are additional and depend on usage.

**Q: Can the AI chatbot learn from conversations?**
A: Yes, we can implement feedback loops and fine-tuning to improve responses over time. This requires ongoing data collection and periodic retraining.

**Q: Is my data secure when using AI?**
A: Yes. We follow best practices: data encryption, secure API calls, GDPR/privacy compliance. We can use self-hosted models or enterprise AI APIs with data residency guarantees if needed.

### Automation

**Q: What tasks can you automate?**
A: Almost anything repetitive: data entry, report generation, email workflows, social media posting, lead routing, invoice processing, appointment scheduling, inventory updates, and more.

**Q: Do I need to buy automation software?**
A: Depends on the project. Tools like Zapier have free tiers, but complex workflows may require paid plans ($20-$200/month). We'll recommend the most cost-effective solution.

**Q: Can you automate tasks across different tools?**
A: Yes. We connect apps via APIs (e.g., Shopify → Google Sheets → Slack → HubSpot). Most modern SaaS tools have APIs or native integrations.

**Q: What if something breaks in my automation?**
A: We include 30-90 days post-launch support. For ongoing monitoring and maintenance, we offer retainer plans starting at $200/month.

### Web Development

**Q: How long does it take to build a website?**
A: Simple landing page: 1-2 weeks. Marketing site with 5-10 pages: 3-6 weeks. Complex web app: 2-4+ months. Timeline depends on scope, content readiness, and revisions.

**Q: Will my website be mobile-friendly?**
A: Yes, always. We build mobile-first responsive sites that work perfectly on all screen sizes (phone, tablet, desktop).

**Q: Can you redesign my existing website?**
A: Yes. We can rebuild your site with a modern tech stack (Next.js, Tailwind) while keeping your content and brand identity.

**Q: Do you provide hosting?**
A: We deploy to Vercel (recommended for Next.js) or other platforms (Netlify, AWS, Cloudflare). Hosting costs are separate (typically $0-$50/month for most sites). We can manage hosting for you or hand it off.

**Q: Will I be able to update the site myself?**
A: Yes. We can integrate a headless CMS (Sanity, Contentful) or MDX-based content system so you can edit text, images, and blog posts without touching code.

**Q: Do you offer SEO services?**
A: We build SEO-optimized sites (fast loading, proper meta tags, structured data, sitemap, etc.). For ongoing SEO strategy and content marketing, we can recommend specialists.

### Language & Localization

**Q: Can you build bilingual websites (English + Persian)?**
A: Yes, we specialize in bilingual sites with full RTL (right-to-left) support for Persian/Farsi. We use next-intl for seamless language switching.

**Q: Do you translate content?**
A: We handle the technical implementation (i18n setup, RTL layout). For professional translation, we can connect you with translators or use translation APIs (DeepL, Google Translate).

- Environment variables for secrets (never committed to Git)

- Input validation and sanitization

### Accessibility
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance (target)
- Semantic HTML
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader tested
- Focus management
- Alt text for images
- Color contrast ratios met

## Chatbot Personality & Tone

### Personality
- **Helpful and professional** — knowledgeable but not overly technical
- **Warm and approachable** — friendly without being casual
- **Bilingual native** — equally fluent in English and Persian
- **Concise** — answers in 2-4 sentences unless detail is requested
- **Honest** — admits limitations, never invents information

### Response Guidelines
- **Match the user's language:** If they write in Persian, respond in Persian. If English, respond in English.
- **Don't assume knowledge:** Explain technical terms briefly if they come up.
- **Provide clear next steps:** "To get started, use the contact form" or "I can connect you with our team — would you like me to?"
- **Stay on brand:** Use our services (AI, automation, web dev) as reference points.
- **Escalate when needed:** If the question is too specific or outside the knowledge base, direct to the contact form or email.

### Example Responses

**User (EN):** "How much does a website cost?"
**Bot (EN):** "Website costs depend on complexity. A simple landing page starts around $1,500-$3,000, while a full web application starts at $5,000+. Want a custom quote? Use the contact form and we'll respond within 24 hours."

**User (FA):** "می‌تونید ربات تلگرام بسازید؟"
**Bot (FA):** "بله! ما ربات‌های تلگرام سفارشی می‌سازیم — هم با پاسخ‌های از پیش تعریف‌شده و هم با هوش مصنوعی. برای جزئیات بیشتر از فرم تماس استفاده کنید یا مستقیم به تلگرام ما پیام بدید."

## Out of Scope / Can't Answer

The chatbot should politely decline or redirect if asked about:
- Legal, financial, or medical advice
- Guarantees on rankings, traffic, or revenue
- Exact timelines without project details
- Work outside our services (graphic design, print, video production, hardware)
- Competitor comparisons or negative comments about other companies
- Internal company operations, salaries, or private info
- Political, religious, or controversial topics

**Polite decline template:**
"That's outside my area, but I can connect you with the team via the contact form — they'll have more insight!"
